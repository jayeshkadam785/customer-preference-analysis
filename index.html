Key Insights

  • Niche cuisines rate higher: Indian, Bar Food, Sandwich, BBQ and Mediterranean top the rating charts (~4.0-4.2 avg) — but with far fewer restaurants (30-150) than the mass-market categories.
  • High volume ≠ high rating: North Indian, Chinese, Fast Food and Mughlai draw the most votes (100K-600K+) but sit near the bottom of the rating scale (~3.2-3.3 avg), suggesting oversaturation and inconsistent quality at scale.
  • The sweet spot: Mediterranean, European and Asian cuisines combine solid ratings (3.9-4.0) with genuinely high vote counts (80K-105K) — the best balance of popularity and quality.
  • Overall market: average rating across all rated restaurants is only 3.44/5, meaning most restaurants sit in the "average" band rather than "excellent."
